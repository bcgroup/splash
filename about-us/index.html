ABOUT US

B&C CONTENT is a Seoul and LA-based talent management & production company, specialized in developing creative crossover opportunities between Hollywood and Korea, with our roster of best-in-class global talent, filmmakers, and media companies.

B&C stands for "Bold & Courageous", which represents our passion to fight for bold & courageous storytelling and storytellers. We are the market-makers & champions of diverse, inclusive, creative collaborations between Hollywood & Asia.
